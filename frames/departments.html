Dr. G. Bakthavatsalam

Dr. G. Bakthavatsalam is a renowned healthcare professional and the founder of KG Hospital in Coimbatore. His dedication and commitment to providing affordable healthcare have made KG Hospital one of the most respected multi-specialty hospitals in South India.

Departments at KG Hospital
  • Cardiology – Comprehensive heart care and advanced cardiac surgeries.
  • Neurology – Diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders.
  • Orthopedics – Joint replacement, sports medicine, and trauma care.
  • Oncology – Cancer diagnosis, chemotherapy, and radiation therapy.
  • Nephrology – Dialysis and kidney-related treatment.
  • Gastroenterology – Liver, pancreas, and digestive system care.
  • Pediatrics – Child healthcare and neonatal services.
  • Gynecology – Women’s health, maternity, and infertility services.
  • Emergency & Trauma – 24/7 emergency and critical care services.
